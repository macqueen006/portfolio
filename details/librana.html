Librana.ai

B2B application, AI-powered library management platform built for institutions across Nigeria.
Live
Problem

Libraries and knowledge institutions across Nigeria operated with outdated, manual processes cataloguing was paper-based, user management was fragmented, and content discovery was essentially non-existent. Institutions had no scalable way to digitize their collections or serve patrons efficiently.

Solution

A B2B SaaS platform built on Laravel & Alpine

Built a full B2B SaaS platform from the ground up. The backend built in Laravel handles multi-tenant institution management, user authentication, catalogue APIs, and subscription billing. Alpine.js powers the reactive frontend without the overhead of a full SPA framework, keeping the UI fast and maintainable. Institutions can onboard, manage their collections, and give patrons a modern digital interface.
