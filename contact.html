faq.

What's your development process?

I start with system design architecture diagrams, database schema, and API contracts before writing a single line of code. This prevents scope creep and keeps delivery clean.

How long does a typical project take?

Simple sites and landing pages: 1-2 weeks. Full-stack web apps with backend: 3-6 weeks depending on scope. I give a clear timeline upfront and stick to it.

Do you work with clients outside Nigeria?

Yes I work remotely with clients globally. I've delivered projects for companies in Slovakia, Nigeria, and beyond. Time zones are not a problem.

What's your preferred stack?

Next.js and React on the frontend, Laravel and PHP, Next.js, Nest.js on the backend, MySQL, Postgres for the database. I also work with TypeScript, Tailwind CSS, Livewire, and Alpine.js.

Do you offer support after launch?

Yes. I'm available for bug fixes, feature additions, and ongoing maintenance after delivery.

How do payments work?

60% upfront, 40% on delivery for fixed-scope projects. For ongoing work, monthly billing at the start of each cycle. I accept bank transfer and international payment methods.

Can I hire you for a single feature or small project?

Yes. Not every project needs a full engagement. If you have a specific problem, get in touch and we'll find the right arrangement.

Are you open to full-time remote roles?

Yes actively looking for remote full-stack developer positions. Available immediately.
